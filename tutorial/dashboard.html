Dashboard

In Arth ERP the dashboard contain graphical analysis, recent sales and purchases.

Graphical Analysis

It is very important to analysis data for a business by which they can understand what is going on in the business and how to plan for the improvement of business. Arth analysis your data and display it by graphically.There are four parts in the graphical analysis of Arth software.

1. Overview Of Sales and Purchases for 7 days

Arth display how many sales and purchases the business make within the 7 days

2. Overview Of Sales and Purchases for 31 days

It shows a graphical analysis of sales and purchases for 30 days

3. No. Of stocks

It graphically display how many stocks the business have in their inventory

4. Stocks below the limit

It graphically display how many below limit stocks the business have in their inventory

Recent Activity

Arth display the recent sales and purchases activity.

1. Recent Sales

It display all the 6 recent sales that made by the business.One can view its details from dashboard by clicking view button

2. Recent Purchases

It display all the 6 recent purchases that made by the business.One can view its details from dashboard by clicking view button
